Seamless Unit Submission

The Unit Tracker enables high school students to submit assignments seamlessly, eliminating the hassle of physical submissions. With a user-friendly interface, students can easily upload their work, ensuring a convenient and efficient process.

Submission History Tracking

Students can effortlessly track their assignment submissions, providing a clear overview of their progress throughout the academic year. This feature promotes accountability and helps students stay organized by keeping all their submission records in one centralized location.

Teacher Management Tools

The Unit Tracker empowers teachers by providing a dedicated space to review and accept student submissions. Teachers can conveniently access and assess assignment submission information, ensuring timely feedback and fostering a productive learning environment.
